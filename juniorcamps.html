JUNIOR CAMPS


Training at our Tennis Center Academy is directed primarily towards players who are trying - break into the international scene and for those who already play international tournaments. Our uncompromising approach provides high quality training in great conditions for all the players striving - get ahead. Each player is unique with his or her strengths and weaknesses, which is why our entire program and our coaching method is directed towards the individual

In optimal conditions we work on improving all important areas of student’s tennis game, both technically and tactically. Training is conducted in groups (max three players on the court), and individually with a trainer. Groups will be formed according - the current quality and age. Our Academy has many junior players in all age groups who play at the Academy year around, so all the camp students will have plenty quality sparring matches while they are at the Tennis Center Academy. Depending on interest, some players could play local tournaments in Serbia.

Time is also spent in the classroom, where we give lectures about the basics of tennis game, various topics related - training, match analysis, tactics, nutrition, as well as video analysis of all the players...

All our coaches were successful tennis players and possess both ITF coaching license as well as the B license of the Serbian Tennis Federation (highest level). They have all worked with top junior and pro players. Our staff also includes various professionals: fitness trainers, physical education teachers, doctors of different specialties, nutritionists...

CAMP DAILY SCHEDULE(example)

Hours Activity
7:00 - 7:30 Waking up, hygiene
7:30 - 8:00 Breakfast and preparation for training
8:00 - 8:30 Stretching and warm up
8:30 - 10:30 Tennis training (work on weaknesses, stroke correction, drills ...)
10:30 - 11:00 Stretching
11.00 - 12.30 Break
12:30 - 13:30 Lunch
14:00 - 16:00 Tennis training (drills, sparing matches, playing points)
16:00 - 17:00 Break
17:00 - 18:30 Fitness training
18:30 - 19:30 Dinner
19:30 - 21:00 Theory (tactics, video analysis, psychology in tennis ...) 3 x per week
21:00 - 22:30 TV, video, relax
22:30 - 7:00 Sleep

This schedule would be the same every day, except for rest times, which are Wednesday and Saturday afternoons and whole day Sunday. All the students will have 20 hours of tennis, 5 hours of fitness and about 3 hours of lectures per week.

Tennis Center Academy will organize sightseeing tours of Belgrade for all the participants in order - get acquainted with the sights of the Serbian capital. The players will also visit the Tennis Association of Serbia, as well as the Novak Djokovic center (where you can see all his trophies).

All meals are specifically made for athletes by our nutritionist.

In case player chooses to be at the Academy year around and go to school in Belgrade, we have three excellent international English-speaking schools we cooperate with, that players can choose from.
