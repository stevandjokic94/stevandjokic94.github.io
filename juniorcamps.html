JUNIOR CAMPS


Training at our Tennis Center Academy is directed primarily towards players who are trying to break into the international scene and for those who already play international tournaments. Our uncompromising approach provides high quality training in great conditions for all the players striving to get ahead. Each player is unique with his or her strengths and weaknesses, which is why our entire program and our coaching method is directed towards the individual

In optimal conditions we work on in improving all important areas of student’s tennis game, both technically and tactically. Training is conducted in groups (max three players on the court), and individually with a trainer. Groups will be formed according to the current quality and age. Our Academy has many junior players in all age groups who play at the Academy year around, so all the camp students will have plenty quality sparring matches while they are at the Tennis Center Academy. Depending on interest, some players could play local tournaments in Serbia.

Time is also spent in the classroom, where we give lectures about the basics of tennis game, various topics related to training, match analysis, tactics, nutrition, as well as video analysis of all the players...

All our coaches were successful tennis players and possess both ITF coaching license as well as the B license of the Serbian Tennis Federation (highest level). They have all worked with top junior and pro players. Our staff also includes various professionals: fitness trainers, physical education teachers, doctors of different specialties, nutritionists...
